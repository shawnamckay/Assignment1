Dog Ownership: The Dilemma

       In an ideal world, I would have 101 dogs just like in the movie 101 Dalmations. However, life is cruel, sleep is necessary and my time is limited. So the unfortunate truth is that I must make a choice about which dog I am able to provide all the love and care it deserves. My narrowed down choices are: Shetland Sheepdog, Basset Hound and Beagle. This website details the pros and cons of each breed.
